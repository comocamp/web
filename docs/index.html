The Collaborative Modeling Camp 2020 June 19th-20th. Soltau, Germany. Participate!

Camp

We are living in great times. After decades of misunderstanding our users, we programmers now have better tools. Collaborative Modeling makes it easy to build the software our users really need.

(And not the software we only thought they would need.)

Among such collaborative modeling methods and meshups are ...

  • Event Storming
  • User Story Mapping
  • Event Modeling
  • Impact Mapping
  • Domain Storytelling
  • Storystorming
  • Example Mapping

... and many more!

The CoMoCamp is the great unconference that gathers practioners of all the different methods as well as the inventors behind and thought leaders behind the tools. We invite all interested community members to be part of it. The camp is not-for-profit and organized by the german-speaking community as well as international experts.
