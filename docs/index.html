Our Camp

We are living in great times. After decades of misunderstanding our users, we programmers now have better tools. Collaborative Modeling makes it easy to build the software our users really need.

(And not the software we only thought they would need.)

Our methods

Some Collaborative Modeling methods and mashups are ...

  • Event Storming
  • User Story Mapping
  • Event Modeling
  • Impact Mapping
  • Domain Storytelling
  • Storystorming
  • Context Mapping
  • Example Mapping

... and many more!

Our mission

The #CoMoCamp 2020 is the unconference reaching out to bring practitioners of different methods together with their inventors and thought leaders. The goal is not to pitch specific tools, but to explore their use cases and their relationships. Furthermore we want to investigate the values holding together the tools we love, want to better understand why a new era of Collaborative Modeling feels so different from what many of us associated with "modeling" in a distant past. Therefore we invite all interested community members to be part of this. Our camp is not-for-profit, not-even-sponsored, and meant to be a lot of fun.

Our team

The #CoMoCamp 2020 is organized by a diverse international community of experts.
