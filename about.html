About

About

The #ComoCamp is the unconference reaching out to bring practitioners of collaborative modeling methods together with their creators and thought leaders.

The idea

The #ComoCamp is the idea of Stefan Priebsch, Martin Schimak, and Henning Schwentner. It was shaped by a broad community of experts.

Organizer

Collaborative Modeling Camp (#ComoCamp) 2023 takes place in Vienna (Austria).

The core-team consists of the following persons

  • Stefan Priebsch
  • Martin Schimak
  • Henning Schwentner
  • Daniel Sack
and would not be possible with all the volunteers, thank you for your support .

It is operated by the non-profit association "Collaborate Vienna - Verein zur Förderung der Zusammenarbeit in der Softwareentwicklung" (ZVR-Zahl 1605397921), Vienna (Austria).
